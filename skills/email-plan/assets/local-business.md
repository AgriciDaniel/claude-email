# Local Business Email Strategy Template

## Business Type Characteristics

- Physical location(s) with service area
- Appointment-based or walk-in customers
- Reviews and reputation critical
- Foot traffic and local search visibility
- Repeat business and word-of-mouth referrals
- Seasonal variations in demand

## List Building Strategy

1. **QR Code on Receipts**
   - Add QR code to printed receipts
   - Link to signup landing page with incentive
   - Track conversions with UTM parameters

2. **Signup at Checkout**
   - iPad/tablet at checkout counter
   - Incentive: 10-15% off next visit, exclusive offers
   - Capture: email, phone (for SMS), birthday (optional)

3. **WiFi Login Portal**
   - Require email for guest WiFi access
   - One-time signup, auto-reconnect on return visits
   - Compliance: explicit opt-in checkbox for marketing emails

4. **Google Business Profile**
   - Add newsletter CTA to business description
   - Link to signup form in posts and updates
   - Feature email signup in photos (signage, window cling)

5. **In-Store Signage**
   - Window clings, table tents, counter signs
   - Clear value proposition (exclusive deals, early access)
   - Short URL or QR code for mobile signup

## Segmentation Plan

| Segment | Criteria | Est. Size | Frequency | Content Type |
|---------|----------|-----------|-----------|--------------|
| New Customers | First 30 days after signup | 15% | 2/week | Welcome, educational, first-visit incentive |
| Repeat Customers | 2+ visits/purchases | 30% | 1-2/week | Loyalty rewards, tips, seasonal offers |
| VIP | Top 20% by spend or frequency | 20% | 2/week | Exclusive perks, early access, VIP events |
| At-risk | 60-90 days no visit | 20% | 1/week | Win-back offers, "we miss you", feedback request |
| Lapsed | 90+ days no visit | 15% | 1/month | Re-engagement, major incentive, survey |

## Priority Automation Sequences

### 1. Welcome Series (3-4 emails)

**Trigger:** Email signup
**Goal:** Introduce business, set expectations, drive first visit

- Email 1 (immediate): Welcome + incentive (10-15% off)
- Email 2 (day 3): Brand story, what makes you different
- Email 3 (day 7): Customer testimonials, social proof
- Email 4 (day 14): Reminder of incentive expiration (if not used)

### 2. Review Request Sequence (1-2 emails)

**Trigger:** Visit/purchase (timing varies by industry)
**Goal:** Gather positive reviews, address issues privately

**Timing by Industry:**
- **Restaurant:** 24 hours after visit (meal fresh in memory)
- **Salon/Spa:** Same day after appointment (same-day freshness)
- **Home Services:** 24-48 hours (project completion confirmation)
- **Retail:** 3-5 days (product usage time)
- **Auto Services:** 1-2 days (service quality check)

**Flow:**
- Email 1: "How was your experience?" (link to review platforms)
- Email 2 (if no response): Gentle reminder with incentive for review

### 3. Appointment Reminder Sequence (2-3 emails)

**Trigger:** Appointment scheduled
**Goal:** Reduce no-shows, improve show-up rate

- Email 1 (7 days before): Appointment confirmation, add to calendar
- Email 2 (24 hours before): Reminder with directions, parking, prep instructions
- Email 3 (2 hours before - optional): Final reminder (SMS may be better)

### 4. Birthday/Loyalty Sequence (1-2 emails)

**Trigger:** Birthday (if collected) or anniversary of first visit
**Goal:** Build relationship, drive visit with special offer

- Email 1 (birthday): Personalized greeting + gift (free item, discount)
- Email 2 (1 year anniversary): Thank you + loyalty reward

### 5. Re-engagement/Win-Back Sequence (3-4 emails)

**Trigger:** 60-90 days no visit
**Goal:** Bring back lapsed customers

- Email 1 (60 days): "We miss you" + small incentive
- Email 2 (75 days): "What can we do better?" + survey
- Email 3 (90 days): Stronger incentive (20-25% off, BOGO)
- Email 4 (120 days): Last chance offer before archive

## Content Calendar

**Email Frequency:** 1-2 emails per week maximum

**Content Mix:**
- Tips/Advice: 40% (how-to, industry tips, seasonal advice)
- Promotions: 30% (limited-time offers, new services, events)
- News/Updates: 20% (new hours, staff, menu/service changes)
- Community: 10% (local events, charity, customer spotlights)

**Monthly Template:**

| Week | Value Email | Promotional Email |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| Week 1 | Industry tip or how-to | New service/product announcement |
| Week 2 | Seasonal advice | Limited-time offer (weekend, holiday) |
| Week 3 | Customer spotlight or testimonial | Event invitation or loyalty reward |
| Week 4 | Behind-the-scenes or staff feature | Monthly special or package deal |

**Seasonal Calendar:**

- **January:** New Year goals, resolutions, fresh starts
- **February:** Valentine's Day (romance, self-care, gifts)
- **March:** Spring cleaning, refresh, renewal
- **April:** Spring promotions, Easter, outdoor season prep
- **May:** Mother's Day, graduation, summer prep
- **June:** Father's Day, summer kickoff, vacation season
- **July:** Independence Day, mid-summer events
- **August:** Back-to-school, end of summer, fall prep
- **September:** Fall season, labor day, autumn activities
- **October:** Halloween, fall festivals, holiday prep
- **November:** Thanksgiving, gratitude, holiday shopping
- **December:** Holiday season, gift guides, year-end thanks

## Platform Recommendations

**Best for Local Business:** Mailchimp or MailerLite

**Mailchimp** ($13-45/month for 500-5000 contacts)
- Pros: Easy to use, robust automation, pre-built templates
- Cons: Price increases with list size, some features paywalled

**MailerLite** ($10-30/month for 500-5000 contacts)
- Pros: Affordable, unlimited emails, drag-and-drop builder
- Cons: Fewer integrations than Mailchimp

**Alternative:** Constant Contact (good for events, local businesses)

## KPI Targets

| Metric | Industry Benchmark | Target |
|--------|-------------------|--------|
| Open Rate | 20-30% (local business) | 25% |
| Click Rate | 2-4% | 3% |
| Conversion Rate | 2-5% (visit/purchase) | 3% |
| Unsubscribe Rate | <0.5% | <0.3% |

**Key Metrics to Track:**
- Offer redemption rate (track with unique codes)

- Repeat visit rate (customer lifetime value)

## Compliance Notes

- **CAN-SPAM (US):** Physical address in footer, clear unsubscribe
- **CASL (Canada):** Express consent required at signup
- **SMS Compliance:** If collecting phone for SMS, separate opt-in required

## Best Practices

1. **Keep it Local:** Reference local events, weather, community news
2. **Personalization:** Use first name, reference past visits/purchases
3. **Mobile-Optimized:** 60-70% of local business emails opened on mobile
4. **Clear CTAs:** Phone number (click-to-call), directions (Google Maps), book online
5. **Timely Sending:** Send during business decision times (Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-11am)
6. **Avoid Over-Emailing:** 1-2/week max; locals will unsubscribe if too frequent
7. **Highlight Reviews:** Feature positive reviews in emails (social proof)
8. **Staff Features:** Introduce team members (builds personal connection)
9. **Behind-the-Scenes:** Show personality (kitchen, workshop, process)
10. **Community Involvement:** Highlight charity work, local partnerships, events
